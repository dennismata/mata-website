Our Story

Proven in Europe.
Built for America.

Mata Tape brings high-performance masking technology from Europe's professional markets to the U.S. commercial painting industry — closing a gap that's been open for decades.

How It Started

Mata Tape is a specialist in high-performance masking for professional users. While the brand is new to the United States, the technology is not: our washi-based solutions originate from years of use and refinement within Europe's professional painting and industrial markets, where they have long been the standard for precision and reliability.

We bring this proven expertise to the U.S. — introducing innovations that are new to the American commercial painting industry but have been trusted by European contractors and industrial applicators for years.

Our production and supply model is built for reliability and nationwide continuity, so contractors can standardize results across teams, regions, and project types without worrying about availability.

The Mata Gold Difference

Commercial painting has advanced rapidly — coatings, spray systems, client expectations — yet the masking tape category has seen little meaningful innovation for decades. That gap impacts daily operations: edge quality, rework, variability across crews, and ultimately customer satisfaction.

Mata Gold is our answer. Engineered from authentic Japanese washi paper with a precision adhesive layer, it delivers ultra-sharp paint lines, residue-free removal for up to six months, and performance that holds up under UV exposure, spray applications, and both water and solvent-based coatings.

One tape handles most surfaces and applications — reducing the number of products your crew needs to carry, simplifying training, and delivering consistent results regardless of who is applying it.
