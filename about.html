How It Started

We started Mata Tape with a simple promise to professionals: your tape should work as hard as you do. The brand is new, but the know-how runs deep. Our founders bring years of hands-on tape expertise combined with real-world operations experience to solve a problem painters have faced for decades — masking that slows you down.

So we built something better. Sharp lines on the first pass, clean removal when you're done, and performance you can count on job after job. No gimmicks, no surprises. Just tape that helps your crew prep once, paint once, and move on.

And because reliability matters, our supply model is built for nationwide continuity — so you can standardize across teams and projects without worrying about availability.

The Mata Gold Difference

Professional painting has advanced rapidly — coatings, spray systems, client expectations — yet the masking tape category has seen little meaningful innovation for decades. That gap affects daily operations: edge quality, rework, variability across crews, and ultimately your reputation.

Mata Gold is our answer. Engineered from authentic Japanese washi paper with a precision adhesive layer, it delivers:

  • Ultra-sharp lines — no paint bleed
  • Clean removal up to 6 months — no damage, no residue
  • Strong, thin, and flexible — no tearing, no lifting
  • UV-resistant — indoor and outdoor
  • Performs under spray — consistent coverage
  • Works on most surfaces

One tape that replaces most of what you use today — fewer products to stock, simpler crew training, and consistent results regardless of who is applying it.
