Returns & Refunds

Last updated: March 2026

Authorization Required

All returns require prior authorization. Please contact our customer service team to obtain a return authorization number before sending any products back.

Inspection & Acceptance

Buyers must inspect all products upon receipt and report any damage or defects within 7 days of delivery. Products not reported within this window are considered accepted.

Condition of Returns

Products must be returned in their original, unopened packaging. Opened or used rolls are not eligible for return unless the product is defective.

Defective Products

If you receive a defective or incorrect product, contact us within 7 days of delivery. We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no cost to you.

Refund Process

Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method. For invoiced orders, a credit note will be applied to your account.

Shipping Costs

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to our error or a defective product.

Partner Orders

Returns for partner program orders (T1P, CPIA, BPN) are subject to the terms outlined in your partner agreement. Contact your partner representative for assistance.

To initiate a return, contact us at support@mata-tape.com with your order number.
