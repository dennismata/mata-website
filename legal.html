Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Mata Tape, Inc. ("Mata Tape," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data when you visit our website or place an order with us.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Account information: name, email address, and password when you create an account.
  • Order information: billing address, shipping address, and order history.
  • Payment information: we do not store card numbers or banking details. All payment data is handled directly by Stripe, Inc. under their own privacy policy.
  • Communications: messages or inquiries you send us via email or contact forms.
  • Usage data: pages visited, browser type, and general location data collected automatically via cookies and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process, fulfill, and ship your orders
  • To create and manage your account
  • To send order confirmations, invoices, and shipping updates
  • To respond to customer service inquiries
  • To administer partner program memberships
  • To improve our website, products, and services
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Payment Processing

All payment transactions — including credit card payments and Net-15 invoice payments — are processed by Stripe, Inc. Mata Tape, Inc. never stores, transmits, or has access to your full card number or banking credentials. Please review Stripe's Privacy Policy for details on how your payment data is handled.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Shipping carriers (e.g., UPS, FedEx) to fulfill and deliver your orders.
  • Stripe, Inc. to process payments and manage invoices.
  • Legal authorities if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights.

5. Partner Programs

If you access our website through a partner program, your order data may be used to prepare reports for the relevant partner organization. Individual identifying information is not shared with partner organizations — only aggregated order totals.

6. Cookies & Analytics

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to remember your preferences, maintain your session, and analyze site traffic. You may disable cookies in your browser settings; however, some features (such as cart persistence and account sessions) may not function correctly without them. We do not use cookies for advertising or cross-site tracking purposes.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Order records are retained for a minimum of seven years to comply with tax and accounting requirements. You may request deletion of your account data at any time, subject to any legal retention obligations.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities
  • Receive a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@mata-tape.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including HTTPS encryption on all pages and access controls on customer data. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us at info@mata-tape.com.

Returns & Refunds

Last updated: March 2026

Authorization Required

All returns require prior authorization. Please contact our customer service team to obtain a return authorization number before sending any products back.

Inspection & Acceptance

Buyers must inspect all products upon receipt and report any damage or defects within 7 days of delivery. Products not reported within this window are considered accepted.

Condition of Returns

Products must be returned in their original, unopened packaging. Opened or used rolls are not eligible for return unless the product is defective.

Defective Products

If you receive a defective or incorrect product, contact us within 7 days of delivery. We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no cost to you.

Refund Process

Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method. For invoiced orders, a credit note will be applied to your account.

Shipping Costs

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to our error or a defective product.

Partner Orders

Returns for partner program orders are subject to the terms outlined in your partner agreement. Contact your partner representative for assistance.

Contact

To initiate a return, contact us at info@mata-tape.com with your order number.
